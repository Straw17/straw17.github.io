Don't Divide Matrices!!!

Dividing Matrices?

When many young math students first learn about matrices, they want to try dividing them. Big mistake! While trying to divide matrices might seem "cool" at first, it's anything but. Dividing matrices can have serious consequences for you and your future.

What the Experts Say

"You can't divide matrices!"
- Bill Daly

"YOU CANNOT"
- Maddox Shrum

"Don't do it man!"
- Jack Shadel

Peer Pressure

Frequently, students face pressure to divide matrices. Other students might claim that dividing matrices is fun, or that you really can divide matrices. Remember that you can always say "no" to dividing matrices. If someone keeps trying to convince you to divide matrices, look them in the eye and say "you can't divide matrices" in a firm voice.

Art

Some artists have recognized the severe danger of dividing matrices, and have made art pieces to discourage dividing matrices.
